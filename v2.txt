ideas

section titles 
- stick to top of screen (on desktop) 
- get smaller the more is scrolled up, unto the minimum size
- use grey for subtitles
- show submenus at the right of section titles (cf. https://www.apple.com/osx/preview/apps/)

section background
- vertical color transition to create distinct edges at the top of a section (cf. https://www.apple.com/osx/preview/apps/)
- parallax scrolling background image as section separators 
  - use css parallax
  - less than 50% of screen height visible
  
- on mobile/touch make each section a separate pane (small distance to border and each other with drop shadow, cf. https://developer.android.com/preview/material/index.html)
